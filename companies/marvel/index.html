MARVEL
From Bankruptcy to Box Office King
December 1996
The Fall
Marvel filed for bankruptcy with $610M in debt. Stock price crashed 93% (from $35.75 to $2.38) as the comic book industry faced a major crisis.
2008
The Gamble
Marvel Studios launched with Iron Man, starring Robert Downey Jr. A risky bet that would change everything.
August 2009
The Acquisition
Disney announced the acquisition of Marvel for $4 billion, recognizing the potential of the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Deal completed in December 2009.
2012
The Breakthrough
The Avengers grossed $1.5+ billion worldwide, proving the MCU concept and setting new box office records.
2025
The Empire
Marvel Cinematic Universe becomes the highest-grossing film franchise ever with $30+ billion in total worldwide box office revenue.
